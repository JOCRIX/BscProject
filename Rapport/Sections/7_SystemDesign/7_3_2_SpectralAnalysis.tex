\subsection{Spectral Analysis} \label{subsec:SpectralAnalysis} 

The Discrete Fourier Transform (DFT) has been implemented to analyze the sampled DUT voltage and current waveform. The DFT has the form shown in equation \refq{eq:4_7_2_SA1}.

\begin{equation}\label{eq:4_7_2_SA1}
    \bar Y = |\bar Y| cos(\phi_1) + j |\bar Y| sin(\phi_1)
\end{equation}
